Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VR fitness

Is VR treadmill training safe?

Absolutely! Our VR treadmills feature comprehensive safety systems including a full-body harness, emergency stop buttons, and trained staff supervision. We maintain a 99% safety record with thousands of sessions completed.

Do I need VR experience to get started?

Not at all! Every session begins with a comprehensive orientation. Our certified trainers will guide you through the equipment setup and basic controls. Most people feel comfortable within the first 5-10 minutes.

What should I wear for a VR treadmill session?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and closed-toe shoes with good grip. We provide clean, sanitized VR headsets and safety harnesses. Avoid loose jewelry or accessories that might interfere with the equipment.

Can I bring friends or work out in groups?

Yes! We offer multiplayer VR experiences where you can run together in the same virtual environment. Group sessions are perfect for team building, friendly competition, or just making workouts more social and fun.

How does the pricing work?

We offer flexible pricing options: single sessions, monthly memberships, and unlimited packages. All plans include equipment use, safety training, and access to our virtual environment library. Check our pricing section for detailed information.

What virtual environments are available?

We have over 50 unique environments including tropical beaches, mountain trails, futuristic cities, fantasy landscapes, and even recreations of famous running routes worldwide. New environments are added monthly based on member feedback.
